
========================
=  Mapper 140          =
========================


Example Game:
--------------------------
Bio Senshi Dan - Increaser Tono Tatakai


Notes:
---------------------------
Regs lie at $6000-7FFF, so there's no SRAM


Registers:
--------------------------
  $6000-7FFF:  [..PP CCCC]
    P = Selects 32k PRG @ $8000
    C = Selects 8k CHR @ $0000
